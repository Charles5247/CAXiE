Contact Form Setup Guide

Step 1: Create a Formspree Account

Go to https://formspree.io and sign up for a free account.

Step 2: Create a New Form

After signing up, create a new form and set the email to: johneme2022@gmail.com

Step 3: Get Your Form Endpoint

Formspree will give you an endpoint like: https://formspree.io/f/xpzgwqjq

Step 4: Update Your Environment Variables

Create a .env file in your project root and add:

REACT_APP_FORMSPREE_ENDPOINT=https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID

Replace YOUR_FORM_ID with your actual Formspree form ID.

Step 5: Test Your Form

Once configured, your contact form will automatically send emails to johneme2022@gmail.com without opening Gmail.

Form Preview (What users will see)

Your contact form will collect:

  • Name
  • Email
  • City
  • Country
  • Subject
  • Message

And send it directly to: johneme2022@gmail.com

Alternative: Quick Setup

If you want to test immediately without setting up Formspree, the form will still work using the mailto fallback (opens Gmail).
